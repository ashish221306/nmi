cross product formula

  • a × b = |a| |b| sin(θ) n
  • |a| is the magnitude (length) of vector a
  • |b| is the magnitude (length) of vector b
  • θ is the angle between a and b
  • n is the unit vector at right angles to both a and b

What is cross product ?

Cross product is vector multiplication of two linear vector in a plane and the product of those linear vector is perpendicular to both of those linear vectors.

What is the cross vector ?

Cross vectors are the vectors which are not parallel to each other . There is some angle between them.
